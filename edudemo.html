What We Offer

Data-driven personalised lessons

Our classes are driven by data to track our students learning ability, which are then fed back into teaching-and-learning activities. This ensures that the lessons are always learner-centered and takes into consideration their strength and weaknesses.

Learning data also provides accurate and seamless reporting to parents to always be in the loop of their children development!

Learn-to-teach approach

While almost all other establishments educate based on the teach-to-learn model, our students are engaged with our very own teach-to-learn framework. Students are not only expected to ace in assessments, coursework and examinations, but are also coached to be able to teach what they learn.

Leadership, self-confidence, presentation skills are not the only takeaways. Apart from getting involved in their own lessons planning with our teachers, the teach-to-learn approach also helps them to lock the knowledge in themselves!
